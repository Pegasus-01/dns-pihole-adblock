      Manual overrides take priority over AI decisions

      ✅ Allowlist

        🚫 Blocklist

          How overrides work:
          Domains in the Allowlist will always be resolved.
          Domains in the Blocklist will always return NXDOMAIN.
          Adding a domain to one list automatically removes it from the other.
          Subdomains inherit the verdict of their parent domain (e.g. blocking doubleclick.net also blocks ads.doubleclick.net).

          Changes are written straight to the database and picked up by the proxy within 30 seconds (its decision cache TTL) — no restart needed.
